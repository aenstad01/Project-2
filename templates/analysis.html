Code Definition Description
OG Original Gravity Density of the unfermented beer (relative to water)
FG Final Gravity Density of the fermented beer (relative to water)
ABV Alcohol By Volume ~As it says~
IBU International Bitterness Units Specifically, 1 IBU = 1 mg of isomerized (exposed to heat) hop alpha acids per liter
BU:GU Bitterness Ratio The ratio of bitterness (IBU) to sugars (Gravity Units, GU)
SRM Standard Reference Method Color, from light to dark
CO2 (v/v) Volumes of CO2 A measure of carbonation
AA Apparent attenuation A measure of malt sugar converted to ethanol during fermentation
